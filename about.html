Ghoulish focuses on real life stories revolving around the supernatural elements of the nature- such as ghosts, ghouls, jinns, etc. The extractor of these tales, who also happens to be the author of this has collected and refined them first hand and they are as authentic as it can get. The blog features a collection of spine-chilling anecdotes that fall under the genre horror, suspense, and mystery. Each narrative holds a detailed description of these real-life horror incidents that people from all over the world have had to experience on different scales. Therefore, the writer of this blog would like to put out a gentle reminder that this website is not for those who are weak at heart. One may experience paranoia, anxiety and delusions after indulging into these stories. We hope you enjoy Ghoulish, and don’t forget to check under your bed before going to sleep tonight.
