Sighting of Slender Man

In 2009, Slender Man was given life in a spooky corner of the Internet called Creepypasta. A supernatural meme created as part of a Photoshop contest by Something Awful forum user Eric Knudsen (screen name Victor Surge), Slender Man is a faceless, spindly specter who stalks children and whose myth has penetrated digital stories, web videos, and now real life. It even inspired a 2018 movie starring Joey King.
